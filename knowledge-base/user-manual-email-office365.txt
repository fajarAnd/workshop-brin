2. OUTLOOK WEB APP (OWA) - BASIC FUNCTIONS
=============================================================================

Compose & Send Email:

Step 1: Click "New message" button (top-left)
Step 2: Fill in:
  - To: Recipient email addresses (multiple separated by semicolon or Enter)
  - Cc: Carbon copy (optional)
  - Bcc: Blind carbon copy (recipients tidak saling visible)
  - Subject: Email subject (concise & descriptive)
  - Body: Email message (formatting toolbar available)

Step 3: Formatting Options:
  - Bold, Italic, Underline
  - Bullet points & numbering
  - Font type & size
  - Text color & highlighting
  - Insert link, table, image

Step 4: Attach Files:
  - Click "Attach" icon
  - Choose file from computer OR OneDrive
  - Max 25 MB total attachment size
  - Large files: Share via OneDrive link instead

Step 5: Click "Send"

Pro Tips:
- Use "Schedule send" untuk delayed sending (handy untuk email luar jam kerja)
- Use "Delay delivery" jika ingin email dikirim di waktu tertentu
- Draft auto-saved setiap beberapa detik

---

Reading & Managing Email:

Inbox Organization:
- Focused Inbox: Prioritized emails (algorithmically determined)
- Other Inbox: Less important emails
- Toggle: Switch between Focused/Other with tabs

Actions on Email:
- Reply: Reply hanya ke sender
- Reply All: Reply ke semua recipients (Be careful! Avoid "reply-all storms")
- Forward: Forward email ke orang lain
- Delete: Move to Deleted Items (recoverable for 30 days)
- Archive: Move to Archive folder (keep inbox clean)
- Flag: Mark email for follow-up
- Categorize: Color-code emails (create custom categories)

Search Email:
- Search box: Top of page
- Search scope: Current folder (default) atau All mailboxes
- Search filters: From, Subject, Has attachments, Date range
- Advanced search: Click search box → More filters
- Save search: For frequently used searches

---

Folders & Organization:

Default Folders:
- Inbox: Incoming emails
- Drafts: Saved drafts
- Sent Items: Sent emails
- Deleted Items: Trash (auto-deleted after 30 days)
- Archive: Long-term storage
- Junk Email: Spam (auto-filtered)
- Clutter: Low-priority emails (deprecated, use Focused Inbox instead)

Create Custom Folders:
1. Right-click "Folders" → "Create new folder"
2. Name the folder (e.g., "Projects", "Vendor Communications")
3. Drag & drop emails to organize

Inbox Rules (Automated Organization):
1. Click Settings (gear icon) → View all Outlook settings
2. Mail → Rules
3. Add new rule:
   - Condition: If email from specific sender, subject contains keywords, etc.
   - Action: Move to folder, categorize, forward, delete, etc.
4. Example: "All emails from boss@brin.go.id → move to 'Important' folder + flag"

3. CALENDAR - SCHEDULING & MEETINGS
=============================================================================

Create Appointment/Event:

Step 1: Go to Calendar (icon on left sidebar)
Step 2: Click "New event" button
Step 3: Fill in:
  - Title: Event name
  - Start/End: Date & time (or toggle "All day")
  - Location: Meeting room, building, or "Microsoft Teams Meeting"
  - Description: Agenda, notes, dial-in info
  - Attendees: Invite people (optional untuk appointments, required untuk meetings)

Step 4: Options:
  - Show as: Free, Busy, Tentative, Out of Office
  - Reminder: Notification before event (15 min, 30 min, 1 hour, etc.)
  - Recurrence: Repeat daily/weekly/monthly (for recurring meetings)
  - Sensitivity: Normal, Private, Personal, Confidential

Step 5: Click "Save" (appointment) atau "Send" (meeting invitation)

---

Schedule Meeting with Others:

Step 1: New event → Add attendees (search by name or email)
Step 2: Check Scheduling Assistant:
  - Click "Scheduling Assistant" tab
  - Shows attendees' free/busy status
  - Suggests best meeting time (when all/most attendees free)
Step 3: Reserve meeting room:
  - Add room resource (e.g., "Meeting Room A-301")
  - System auto-checks room availability
  - Room auto-accepted if available
Step 4: Add Microsoft Teams Meeting:
  - Toggle "Teams meeting" switch
  - Automatic Teams link generated & added to invite
Step 5: Click "Send" to invite attendees

Attendee Actions:
- Accept: Attending, added to calendar
- Tentative: Maybe attending
- Decline: Not attending
- Propose new time: Suggest alternate time (organizer can accept/reject)

---

Meeting Room Booking:

Available Meeting Rooms (BRIN Jakarta):
- Meeting Room A-301 (Capacity: 10 people, Projector, Whiteboard)
- Meeting Room A-401 (Capacity: 20 people, Video Conference, Projector)
- Meeting Room B-201 (Capacity: 6 people, TV screen)
- Conference Hall (Capacity: 100 people, AV system, catering available)

How to Book:
1. Create meeting invitation
2. Add room as "Room" (not attendee): Search "Meeting Room A-301"
3. If room free, auto-accepted; if busy, declined
4. Alternative: Use "Scheduling Assistant" to find available room

Room Etiquette:
- Cancel booking if meeting cancelled (free up room for others)
- End meeting on time (respect next booking)
- Clean up room after use
- Report issues (AV not working, room dirty) to Facilities via ext. 190

---

Calendar Views:

View Options:
- Day: Single day view (hourly grid)
- Work week: Monday-Friday (default)
- Week: Monday-Sunday (include weekends)
- Month: Calendar month view
- Agenda: List view of upcoming events

Calendar Sharing:
1. Settings → Calendar → Shared calendars
2. Share your calendar:
   - Choose person/group to share with
   - Permission level:
     * Can view when I'm busy: See free/busy only
     * Can view titles and locations: See event names
     * Can view all details: Full event details
     * Can edit: Modify events
     * Delegate: Full control + can send meetings on your behalf
3. View someone else's shared calendar:
   - If shared with you, appears in "Shared calendars" section
   - Or: Add calendar → Search person's name → Add

5. ONEDRIVE FOR BUSINESS - FILE STORAGE & SHARING
=============================================================================

OneDrive Basics:

Storage Capacity: 1 TB per user

Access OneDrive:
- Web: https://onedrive.com (auto-redirects to BRIN tenant)
- Desktop: OneDrive sync client (pre-installed on BRIN laptops)
- Mobile: OneDrive app (iOS/Android)

Upload Files:
- Web: Click "Upload" → Select files or folders
- Desktop sync: Copy files to OneDrive folder (auto-syncs to cloud)
  - Default location: C:\Users\[username]\OneDrive - BRIN

Sync Client Setup (if not installed):
1. Download from https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/onedrive/download
2. Install and sign in with BRIN email
3. Choose folders to sync (or sync all)
4. Files sync automatically (two-way sync)

---

Sharing Files & Folders:

Share a File:
1. Right-click file → Share
2. Options:
   - Specific people: Enter email addresses (internal or external)
   - Anyone with the link: Public link (use cautiously!)
   - People in BRIN: Anyone in organization
3. Permission:
   - Can view: Read-only
   - Can edit: Modify & download
4. Set expiration date (optional)
5. Add message (optional)
6. Click "Send" (email invitation) or "Copy link" (share manually)

Share a Folder:
- Same process as file
- Shared folder appears in recipient's "Shared with me" section
- Permissions apply to all files in folder

Revoke Access:
1. Right-click file/folder → Manage access
2. See list of people with access
3. Click "X" next to person to remove access

---

Version History & Recovery:

Version History:
- OneDrive auto-saves versions of files
- Retention: Keep all versions for 30 days (or until storage quota exceeded)
- Access: Right-click file → Version history
- Restore: Select previous version → Restore

Recover Deleted Files:
- Deleted files go to Recycle Bin
- Retention: 30 days in Recycle Bin
- Recover: Go to Recycle Bin → Select file → Restore
- Permanently delete: Empty Recycle Bin (cannot undo!)

Ransomware Recovery:
- If files encrypted by ransomware, use "Restore your OneDrive"
- Settings → Restore your OneDrive → Select date before infection
- Restores entire OneDrive to previous state

6. MICROSOFT TEAMS - COLLABORATION & CHAT
=============================================================================

Teams Overview:

Teams vs Channels vs Chats:
- Team: Group of people working together (e.g., "IT Department", "Research Project X")
- Channel: Topic-based conversations within a Team (e.g., "General", "Announcements", "Tech Support")
- Chat: 1-on-1 or group chat (informal, quick messages)

Access Teams:
- Web: https://teams.microsoft.com
- Desktop: Teams desktop app (recommended)
- Mobile: Teams app (iOS/Android)

---

Chat (1-on-1 or Group):

Start a Chat:
1. Click "Chat" icon (left sidebar)
2. Click "New chat" button
3. Add people (type name or email)
4. Type message → Enter to send

Chat Features:
- Rich text formatting (bold, italic, bullet points)
- @Mention: Type @ + name to notify specific person
- Emoji & GIFs: Express yourself
- Attach files: From computer or OneDrive
- Audio/Video call: Click call icon to start call

---

Teams & Channels:

Join a Team:
1. Click "Teams" icon → Browse or join a team
2. Search for team name (if public)
3. Request to join (if private, owner must approve)

Create a Team (if authorized):
1. Click "Teams" icon → Create team
2. Choose template (Standard, from scratch, or from existing group)
3. Privacy: Private (invite-only) or Public (anyone can join)
4. Add members
5. Create channels for different topics

Post in Channel:
1. Go to Team → Select channel
2. Click "New post" or reply to existing post
3. Type message → Post
4. Use @Team name to notify all members
5. Use @Channel name to notify all channel members

---

Meetings in Teams:

Schedule Meeting:
1. Go to Calendar → New meeting
2. Add attendees, date/time, Teams meeting (auto-enabled)
3. Send invitation

Join Meeting:
- Click "Join" in calendar event
- Or click meeting link in email/chat
- Choose audio/video settings → Join now

Meeting Controls:
- Mic: Mute/unmute (Ctrl+Shift+M)
- Camera: Video on/off (Ctrl+Shift+O)
- Share screen: Share desktop, window, or PowerPoint
- Chat: Meeting chat (visible to all participants)
- Participants: See who's in meeting
- Leave: End your participation (or End meeting if organizer)

---

File Sharing in Teams:

Files in Channels:
- Each channel has "Files" tab
- Stored in SharePoint (backed by OneDrive)
- Collaboration: Multiple people can edit simultaneously
- Version history & recovery available

Share File in Chat:
- Click "Attach" icon → Upload from computer or OneDrive
- File shared with chat participants
- Recipients can view/edit (depending on permissions)

7. SECURITY & BEST PRACTICES
=============================================================================

Email Security:

Identify Phishing Emails:
- Suspicious sender (misspelled domain, generic email)
- Urgent action required (threat, time pressure)
- Spelling & grammar errors
- Suspicious links (hover to see real URL)
- Unexpected attachments (especially .exe, .zip, .scr)

What to Do:
- DO NOT click links or download attachments
- DO NOT reply or provide information
- Report as phishing: Click "Report message" → Phishing
- Delete email
- If clicked: Contact IT Security immediately (security@brin.go.id or ext. 911)

---

Attachment Security:

Safe File Types:
- Documents: .pdf, .docx, .xlsx, .pptx
- Images: .jpg, .png, .gif
- Archives: .zip (scan before extracting)

Dangerous File Types (BLOCKED by email filter):
- Executables: .exe, .com, .bat, .cmd, .scr
- Scripts: .js, .vbs, .ps1
- Macros: .docm, .xlsm (allowed but scanned)

Best Practices:
- Scan attachments with antivirus before opening (automatic on BRIN laptops)
- Verify sender if unexpected attachment
- Large files: Share via OneDrive link instead of attachment

---

Password & Security:

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- Complexity: Uppercase + lowercase + numbers + symbols
- Change every 90 days
- Cannot reuse last 10 passwords

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):
- Enabled for all BRIN accounts
- Setup: https://aka.ms/mfasetup
- Methods: Microsoft Authenticator app (recommended), SMS (backup)
- Approve MFA prompt only if you initiated the login!

Account Security:
- Never share password with anyone (including IT support)
- Lock computer when away (Windows+L)
- Sign out from shared/public computers
- Report suspicious account activity immediately

---

Data Classification & Handling:

Email Classification:
- Use subject tags: [PUBLIC], [INTERNAL], [CONFIDENTIAL], [RESTRICTED]
- Automatic: Sensitivity labels in Outlook (Apply → Choose label)

Sharing Sensitive Data:
- CONFIDENTIAL: Encrypt email (Options → Encrypt) or share via secure OneDrive link
- RESTRICTED: Do not send via email, use encrypted file share or hand-deliver
- External sharing: Get manager approval for CONFIDENTIAL+

Data Retention:
- Email auto-archived after 2 years (moved to Online Archive)
- Deleted items purged after 30 days
- Legal hold: Some mailboxes have retention policies (cannot delete emails)

8. TROUBLESHOOTING - COMMON ISSUES
=============================================================================

Issue 1: Cannot send/receive emails

Troubleshooting:
1. Check internet connection (ping 8.8.8.8)
2. Check if Outlook online accessible (https://outlook.office.com)
3. If desktop Outlook: Click "Send/Receive All Folders"
4. Check mailbox quota (File → Mailbox Cleanup → View mailbox size)
   - If >95% full, delete old emails or archive
5. If still issue, contact IT Support

---

Issue 2: Emails going to Junk/Spam

Troubleshooting:
1. Check Junk Email folder
2. Mark as "Not Junk" → Right-click email → Junk → Not Junk
3. Add sender to Safe Senders list:
   - Settings → View all settings → Mail → Junk email
   - Add sender's email to Safe senders and domains
4. If legitimate business email blocked, contact IT to whitelist

---

Issue 3: Meeting room not accepting booking

Troubleshooting:
1. Check room calendar (search room name → Open calendar)
2. Verify room available at requested time
3. Try different time slot
4. If room shows "Free" but declines: Contact Facilities (room might be offline for maintenance)
5. Alternative: Book different room

---

Issue 4: Cannot access shared OneDrive file

Troubleshooting:
1. Verify link not expired (check with sender)
2. Try opening in incognito/private browser window
3. Check if file requires BRIN login (might need VPN if remote)
4. Request access: Click "Request access" button → Owner will receive notification
5. If still issue: Ask owner to re-share with your email address

---

Issue 5: Teams meeting audio/video not working

Troubleshooting:
1. Check device permissions:
   - Teams → Settings → Permissions
   - Allow microphone & camera access
2. Test devices:
   - Teams → Settings → Devices → Test call
   - Check if mic picking up sound, camera showing video
3. Check if muted in meeting (red mic icon)
4. Restart Teams app
5. Update Teams to latest version
6. If still issue: Join via web browser (https://teams.microsoft.com) as fallback

10. TIPS & PRODUCTIVITY HACKS
=============================================================================

Keyboard Shortcuts (Outlook Web):

- New email: Ctrl+Shift+M (Cmd+Shift+M on Mac)
- Reply: Ctrl+R
- Reply all: Ctrl+Shift+R
- Forward: Ctrl+Shift+F
- Send email: Ctrl+Enter
- Search: Ctrl+Alt+K
- Go to Calendar: Ctrl+2
- Go to People: Ctrl+3

---

Email Management Tips:

1. Inbox Zero Strategy:
   - Process emails once or twice daily (don't check constantly)
   - Decide immediately: Delete, Archive, Reply, Defer (flag for follow-up)
   - Use folders sparingly (search is powerful)

2. Use Quick Steps (automated actions):
   - Create custom quick steps: Settings → Quick steps
   - Example: "Move to Project X folder + Mark as read" (1-click action)

3. Email Templates:
   - For repetitive emails (e.g., out-of-office reply, meeting confirmation)
   - Compose → My templates → Save as template
   - Reuse: Compose → My templates → Select template

4. Clutter/Sweep Rules:
   - Auto-delete/archive newsletters older than 10 days
   - Right-click email → Sweep → Choose action

5. Focused Inbox Training:
   - Move emails between Focused ↔ Other to train algorithm
   - System learns your priorities over time

---

Calendar Productivity:

1. Color-code Calendars:
   - Personal, work projects, team meetings (different colors)
   - Quick visual scanning of day

2. Time Blocking:
   - Block focus time on calendar (mark as "Busy")
   - Colleagues see you're unavailable → fewer meeting invites

3. Meeting Policies:
   - Default meeting: 25 min (not 30) or 50 min (not 60) → buffer time
   - Settings → Calendar → Events → Shorten appointments and meetings

4. Calendar Analytics:
   - MyAnalytics (if enabled): Insights on meeting time, email habits
   - Access: https://myanalytics.microsoft.com

---

Teams Productivity:

1. Custom Status:
   - Set status: Available, Busy, Do not disturb, Be right back, Away, Offline
   - Custom message: Add context ("In meeting until 3pm", "Focused work - urgent only")

2. Keyboard Shortcuts (Teams):
   - Ctrl+E: Search (people, messages, files - universal search bar)
   - Ctrl+Shift+M: Mute/unmute in meeting
   - Ctrl+Shift+O: Video on/off
   - Ctrl+Shift+X: Hand raise (in meeting)

3. @Mentions Best Practices:
   - @Person: Notify individual
   - @Team: Notify entire team (use sparingly!)
   - @Channel: Notify all channel members
   - Avoid @mention abuse (notification fatigue)

4. Pin Important Chats/Channels:
   - Right-click chat/channel → Pin
   - Pinned items always at top of list
